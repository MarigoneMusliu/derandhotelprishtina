The Art of the Hand & Foot

Bespoke Nail Care & Restorative Therapies

At RINU MEDSPA, we transform routine nail care into a high-end wellness ritual. Our Manicure and Pedicure suites are designed for those who seek perfection in every detail—combining medical-grade hygiene with the ultimate in relaxation and aesthetic artistry.

1. Signature Manicure Collection

More than just a polish; a complete rejuvenation for your hands.

Bespoke Russian Manicure

Our specialty. A meticulous, dry-machine technique that focuses on perfect cuticle health and precision, providing a clean, "high-definition" look that lasts longer than traditional methods.

Structured Gel & Strengthening

We use premium, self-leveling gels to create a natural, durable apex on your nails. This provides strength and a flawless "C-curve" without the bulk of acrylics.

Anti‑Aging Hand Treatment

Hands are the first to show age. We incorporate gentle chemical peels and deep‑hydration paraffin masks to reduce sun spots and restore skin elasticity.

2. Advanced Pedicure Rituals

Step into a world of comfort with treatments designed for both beauty and podiatric health.

The Med‑Pedi Experience

A clinical approach to beauty. We use specialized tools to safely address calluses, dry heels, and nail health, followed by a luxurious aesthetic finish.

Detoxifying Soaks & Exfoliation

Our pedicures begin with a mineral-rich soak infused with essential oils, followed by a manual sugar‑scrub exfoliation to improve circulation and skin texture.

Deep Tissue Foot Massage

Every pedicure at RINU includes a targeted pressure‑point massage to relieve the tension of travel and daily stress, leaving you feeling light and restored.

3. Aesthetic Finishes & Artistry

Express your style with our curated palette of luxury finishes.

The Boutique Palette

A hand‑picked selection of the season's most sophisticated shades, from timeless "Derand Nudes" to bold, high‑fashion pigments.

Minimalist Nail Art

For the modern professional, we offer subtle, elegant designs—think ultra‑fine lines, chrome finishes, and understated geometric accents.

Breathable & Vegan Options

We prioritize nail health with high‑quality, non‑toxic polishes that allow the nail bed to remain hydrated and strong.

The RINU Standard of Hygiene

"Your safety is our luxury. Every instrument used in our Manicure and Pedicure sessions undergoes a Medical‑Grade Autoclave Sterilization process, and all single‑use items are disposed of immediately. We provide a pristine environment where you can truly let go."
